VeeR Academy

CLIENT OVERVIEW

"We have seen more and more people started to get interested in creating 360 videos, however, quite a few of them is still new to the process and techniques of making a good 360 video. They can be totally new to VR but curious about finding out how it works, or just brought their first 360 camera but still figuring out to make nice 360 videos.As the global VR video community, we want to help these creators by providing them a centralized place for them to learn these skills and exchange knowledge." - VeeR VR


DESIGN TASK

VeeR VR hopes to create an online tutorial platform for 360 video by collecting creating instructions for its upcoming creators. In the current website, VR tutorial is just a sub-page under the Blog.

DESIGN PROCESS

Brainstorm & Affinity Map

Through group brainstorm and votes for later affinity map, we achieved our design solutions as follows:

Homepage

1.Display sample works of courses in the heading position to attract users and prolong their stay.

2.Design a filter for searching courses by both contents and levels.

3.Show clear course lists especially including Sample Works and Course Abstract.


Course Page

1.Design two learning modes including the scrolling-down mode and the screen one for users to switch.

2.Design a course syllabus canvas for users to skip anywhere quickly.

3.Design easy pathways to Course Project & Sample Works / Q&A / Related Courses respectively.
